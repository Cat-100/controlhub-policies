# 1. What Information Do We Collect?

## Personal information you disclose to us

We collect personal information that you provide to us.

- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Names
- Usernames
- Passwords
- Billing addresses
- Contact or authentication data

**Sensitive Information:** We do not process sensitive information.

## Information automatically collected

We collect certain information automatically when you visit or use our Services.

- **Log and Usage Data:** Includes IP address, browser type, device data, etc.
- **Location Data:** May include geolocation data based on IP address or GPS.

## Google API

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
